CAVE Loop 2 and TLB Requirements
Dangun Feveron
To get the TLB, No Miss up to the Stage 5 boss

Deathsmiles Mega Black Label
To get Bloody Jitterbug in v1.0, meet ALL of the following requirements:
  • Play on Lv.999
  • Select and No Miss Gorge and Ice Palace
  • No Miss until the end of Jitterbug

    • Bloody Jitterbug has no requirements in v1.1.

    DonPachi
    This game has no Loop 2 or TLB requirements, Loop 2 will automatically start after Loop 1, even if contniues are used. The TLB will always show up at the end of Loop 2.

    DoDonPachi
    To access Loop 2, meet any of the following requirements in the first loop:
  • No more than 2 miss
  • 270 hit chain (Type A)
  • 300 hit chain (Type B)
  • 330 hit chain (Type C)
  • At least 50,000,000 points
  • Bee Perfect in at least 4 stages
    •
    DoDonPachi DaiOuJou
    To access Loop 2, meet any of the following requirements in the first loop:
  • No more than 2 miss
  • No more than 3 bombs used
  • Bee Perfect in at least 3 stages
  • At least 350,000,000 points (White Label only)
    •
    DoDonPachi DaiFukkatsu
    To access the Omote Loop, meet any of the following requirements in the first loop:
  • At least 35 bees collected
  • No more than 2 bombs used, including autobombs
  • No more than 1 miss (Power Style only)

    • To access the Ura Loop, meet ALL of the following requirements in the first loop:
  • All 45 bees collected
  • No more than 2 bombs used, including autobombs
  • No more than 1 miss (Power Style only)
    •
    DoDonPachi DaiFukkatsu Black Label
    To get Hibachi, meet ALL of the following requirements:
  • Defeat all bosses with full Red gauge
  • No more than 2 bombs used before Taisabachi

    • To get Zatsuza, meet ALL of the following requirements:
  • Use Strong Style
  • Defeat all bosses with full Red gauge
  • No more than 2 bombs used before Taisabachi
  • No miss after defeating Taisabachi
    •
    DoDonPachi SaiDaiOuJou
    To get Hibachi, meet ALL of the following requirements:
  • Bee Perfect in 3 stages
  • No more than 1 miss

    • To get Inbachi, meet ALL of the following requirements:
  • All 40 bees collected
  • No Miss No Bomb
  • Rank 30 or higher
  • Rank 50 (Expert Mode only)
    •
    Espgaluda II
    To get Shin Seseri, No Miss up to the Stage 5 boss

    Ketsui: Kizuna Jigoku Tachi
    To access the Omote Loop, clear Loop 1 with a miss and bomb total of 6 or less

    To access the Ura Loop, meet ALL of the following requirements in the first loop:
  • No Miss No Bomb
  • At least 120,000,000 points
    •
    Muchi Muchi Pork
    To access Loop 2, No Miss the first loop. 1 Miss is allowed if you get the secret extend.

    Mushihimesama Futari Black Label
    To get Spiritual Larsa, meet ALL of the following requirements:
  • Play on God Mode
  • No Miss
    •
    Progear
    To access Loop 2, meet any of the following requirements in the first loop:
  • No more than 1 miss
  • No more than 2 bombs used
  • Combined misses/bombs is 7 or less
